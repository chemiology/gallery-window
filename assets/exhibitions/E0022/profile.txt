Since 2015, Jungran Lee has been presenting delicate drawings made with soft materials such as colored pencils and pencils on the smooth surface of acrylic plates. She tries to maintain extreme immersion and intentional inner peace by repeatedly drawing thin lines across the entire surface of the acrylic plate without using a ruler. As a result, the process of creation brings contemplation and mental relaxation. Jungran Lee conveys the inner peace that exists beyond her work to others, expressing consolation, sympathy, and solidarity.<br><br><br>
