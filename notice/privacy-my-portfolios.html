My Portfolios Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

English

My Portfolios respects the privacy of users.

This application does not collect, store, transmit, or share personal information with external servers.

Information Access

My Portfolios may access local device storage only when selected directly by the user.

  • ZIP files selected by the user
  • Portfolio folders selected by the user
  • Image and audio files used for exhibitions
  • SD card folders selected for backup purposes

All file access is performed locally on the device. No files are uploaded to external servers.

Data Sharing

My Portfolios does not share user data with third parties.

External Websites

The application may open external websites selected by the user, including Gallery Window and PhotoBase.

These websites operate independently from the application.

Children's Privacy

My Portfolios is not directed toward children under 13.

Website: https://gallerywindow.com
